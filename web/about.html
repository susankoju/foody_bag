About Us

In 2019, we started off as the first specialty franchise in Nepal to specialize in handcrafted cusine . Over the years, we have diversified from a small franchise to an international franchising Nepalese Cusine Brand with outlets around the world. We believe in maintaining a sustainable business ecosystem by supporting local farmers and Nepali products. Through continuous innovation in technology & process, we hope to become the most inspiring Nepalese Cusine Establishment in the world taking Nepalese Cusine and Culture to the world.
